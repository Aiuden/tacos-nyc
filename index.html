About

New York City is home to some of the best tacos in the world — and we're on a mission to find them all. This site is a personal project to rank and track every taco spot across the five boroughs.

Each spot is rated across three categories: Taco (tortilla quality, protein, overall construction), Toppings (salsa, onion, cilantro, and any extras), and Store (vibe, cleanliness, service, and value). Scores are on a 1–5 scale with one decimal point. Only tacos are evaluated — no burritos, no quesadillas.

All visits are anonymous and self-funded. No sponsored content, ever.
